Note on the Text

My Bondage and My Freedom was first published in New York, NY, and Auburn, NY, by Miller, Orton & Mulligan, in 1855. The text of this ebook edition is based on Project Gutenberg files and scans of the first edition held by Bringham Young University, available on Archive.Org. While every effort has been made to ensure that the text is reproduced faithfully, this edition is no substitute for an authoritative edition.
